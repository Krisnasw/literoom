RCAM - Rosyid College of Arts & Maritime Studies Branding, Graphic Design, Web Design

Rosyid college of arts & Maritime Studies

is the school of arts and maritime which is located in Surabaya. We focus on the development of arts and maritime studies program. The aim of our practice is to provide students the knowledge of experts in arts and maritime before entering their next level of education.

We also produce a trusted identity and qualify brand through the visual design which are integrated to the combination of arts and maritime. We concern more in the uniqueness of our logo and additional visual elements to create a new and fresh look.
